Unit Parts:
Move: Reduces fatigue and allows units to move
    - For every non move part that exists on a unit, increase fatigue by 1
    - For every move part, decrease fatigue by 1
Carry: Gives carry capacity
    - For every carry part, give 10 carry capacity
Melee: For every melee part give 1 melee damage

Ranged: For every ranged part give 1 ranged damage

Heal: For every heal part, gain 10 Heal points

Network: Gives 10 Network points
    - Network points can be either allocated into range or maximum unit count controllable
    - There must be a unit with a network part alive
